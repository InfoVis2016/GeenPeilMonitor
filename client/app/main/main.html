So what is it?

GeenPeil is an initiative to hold a referendum in The Netherlands about the European Union Association Agreement with Ukraine.

The initiative gathered more than 300000 autographs of the dutch people - enough to initiate a referendum. The referendum will be held on the sixth of april 2016 accordingly.

What does it do?

The Association Agreement allows Ukraine to more actively participate with member countries in the European Union.

This means more cooperation on military and economical aspects.

Why should you care?

Do you want more democracy or do you want everything to be decided for you?

Do you want more or less ties with Ukraine?

Do you see Russia as a bully or as an ally?

If these topics are of concern to you, do yourself a favor and vote on the 6th!

The number of voting offices has been decreased

GeenPeil supporters say the governmet is actively working against GeenPeil, and has summoned municipalities who have decreased the number of voting offices [1].

The government says this is due to a lower expected attendance [2].
