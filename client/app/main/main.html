So what is it?

GeenPeil, an initiative to hold a referendum in The Netherlands about the European Union (EU) Association Agreement. The Association Agreement allows Ukraine to more actively participate with member countries in the European Union. As stated in EU law, the referendum should take place as the initiative gathered more than 300000 autographs of the dutch people. The referendum will be held on the sixth of april 2016 accordingly.

The future of Europe

Do you want more democracy or do you want everything to be decided for you?

Do you want more or less ties with Ukraine?

Do you see Russia as a bully or as an ally?

If these topics are of concern to you, do yourself a favor and vote on the 6th!

Currently, the dutch people are undecided.

But what are they talking about?

A staggering amount of 15000 tweets about geenpeil have therefore been recorded in the month before the referendum.
